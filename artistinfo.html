Spotlight: Karma Lama

ThangkaMandalaTraditional

Karma Lama

Boudhanath, Kathmandu · 18 years

Karma Lama was initiated into the thangka tradition at age 12. His work fuses centuries-old iconographic rules with a sensitivity to light rarely seen in traditional Tibetan art. Each painting takes between three months to a year to complete.

"Every brushstroke is a meditation. The painting teaches me as much as I teach it."
